TTRPGs (Tabletop Role-Playing Games)

Games like Dungeons & Dragons where players gather around a table to tell collaborative stories.

D&d beyond is a site that brings together all the official books in the range, in html format. It also manages character sheets and has its own VTT, which for the moment is very minimalist, before moving on to the full 3d version due to arrive later.
